3. How It Is Used

Used exclusively to:

  • Read and respond to your message
  • Detect and prevent spam, bots, and abuse
  • Maintain a contact record for follow-up
  • Understand in general terms how visitors reach the site (non-commercial)

Your information is never sold, rented, or traded. Never used for advertising, retargeting, or marketing. Never shared with data brokers or analytics platforms.

4. Third-Party Services

Formspree

Form submissions are processed by Formspree. All submitted data (including metadata) is transmitted to their servers before delivery to me. Their Privacy Policy governs their handling of that data.

ipapi.co

Geographic and network context is fetched from ipapi.co. This exposes your IP to their servers. Their Privacy Policy applies.

Google Fonts

Typography is served by Google Fonts. Google may receive basic request data including your IP. See Google's Privacy Policy.

No analytics, tracking scripts, ad networks, or other third-party services are used.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

This Site does not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising. sessionStorage is used locally in your browser for rate limiting on form submissions. It never leaves your browser and is cleared when you close the tab. No persistent cookies. No tracking pixels.

6. Data Retention

Messages are retained as long as reasonably necessary to respond and follow up. To request deletion, use the contact form. One person. No bureaucracy. It will be handled promptly.

7. Your Rights

  • Access to personal data held about you

  • Object to or restrict processing

  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (e.g. the ICO in the UK, relevant DPA in the EU)

To exercise any right, use the contact form.

8. Legal Basis (GDPR)

  • Consent — by checking the agreement box, you explicitly consent to collection and processing described here.
  • Legitimate interests — security measures (honeypot, rate limiting, timing) are necessary to protect the Site from abuse.

You may withdraw consent at any time by requesting data deletion.

9. Security

  • HTTPS encryption on all data in transit
  • Four honeypot fields catching automated bot submissions
  • Timing analysis rejecting submissions completed too quickly
  • Client-side rate limiting via sessionStorage
  • Server-side CAPTCHA verification via Formspree
  • Per-field input validation before submission

10. Children's Privacy

This Site is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 where applicable). Contact me to remove any such data without delay.
